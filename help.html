User guide

Submit a case online

  1. Open the service catalogue on the home page.
  2. Pick the service that fits your need.
  3. Click Apply, fill in the form, attach any documents, then submit.
  4. Track progress under My Requests.

Follow up on a case

  1. Sign in to access My Requests.
  2. Switch between Submitted by me and Assigned to me.
  3. Open a case to see the chat-style timeline and respond.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

Click Sign in at the top right and use your VJU institutional Google account.

Can I cancel a submitted case?

Open the case from My Requests and use the "Dismiss request…" option from the more-actions menu.

Why don't I see some internal workflows?

Internal workflows are visible only to staff with deploy permissions for that workflow.
